# James Chen: a vision journey (1999-2026)

An interactive timeline of James Chen's two decades of work on global vision correction -
from Adlens and Adaptive Eyewear, through Vision for a Nation's work in Rwanda and Ghana,
the Clearly campaign, the UN resolution 'Vision for Everyone', and the research programme
(PROSPER, DRIVE, ENGINE) that put glasses on the global development agenda.

**Live page:** open `index.html`, or view via GitHub Pages once enabled.

- Click any milestone to read its story; the timeline reflows around open cards.
- Use the category checkboxes to filter (e.g. just the Clearly campaign, or just the research).
- The (i) button on each open card shows key facts and the source for that milestone.

Compiled by Graeme MacKenzie. Generated from `gMacKenzieMasterTimeline.xlsx`; every date is
source-verified (Companies House filings, signed MOUs, journal DOIs, contemporaneous minutes).
